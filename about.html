Our Story

We Started With Cake

And our clients kept asking: "Can you do the whole party?" So we said yes.

The Origin

The Whole Party is a sister brand of My Baking Creations, the Bay Area's trusted custom bakery since 2012. For over a decade, we've been delivering show-stopping cakes, hand-decorated cookies, and custom desserts to families and Fortune 500 companies across the Bay Area.

Every week, while delivering cakes to birthday parties, we'd see the same thing: parents juggling four different vendors for bounce houses, decorations, tableware, and setup. They'd tell us, "We wish you guys did everything." Eventually, we listened.

Why Us

We're not a faceless rental company. We're the same team that hand-pipes your kid's name on cookies and sculpts 3D cakes that fool your guests. That obsessive attention to detail? It goes into your balloon arches, your table setup, your party favors.

One vendor. One point of contact. One invoice. We handle the logistics so you can handle being a host — or better yet, just enjoy the party.

The Team

Yana — Artistic lead and founder of My Baking Creations. Professional baker, cake designer, and the reason our corporate clients include Google, Meta, Salesforce, DocuSign, and Alaska Airlines. She sets the quality bar.

George — Operations and logistics. If Yana is the artist, George is the architect. He makes sure your bounce house shows up on time, your setup is flawless, and your quote is fair.

Where We Serve

We cover the San Francisco Bay Area: San Francisco, Daly City, San Mateo, Hillsborough, Palo Alto, Mountain View, San Jose, Sausalito, San Rafael, and all of Marin County and the Peninsula. If you're in the Bay, we're there.
